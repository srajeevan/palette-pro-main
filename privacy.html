Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 8, 2026

Palette Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Review & Functionality

We do not collect personal data for marketing purposes. We collect the following for app functionality:

  • Images: Photos you upload solely for the purpose of color analysis. These are processed locally or temporarily for analysis and are not sold to third parties.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous metrics (e.g., button taps, crash logs) to improve app stability.

B. Account Information

If you create an account, we store:

  • Your email address (for authentication via Supabase).
  • Your saved color palettes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to:

  • Provide the core features of the app (Color Mixing, Palette Generation).
  • Sync your data across devices.
  • Respond to customer support requests.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for infrastructure:

  • Supabase: For secure database and authentication hosting.
  • RevenueCat: To manage Pro subscriptions.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account at any time. You can do this directly within the app by going to Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove your personal data and saved palettes from our servers.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
